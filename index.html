Crystal Collector

At the start of the game you will be given a random number.
There will be 4 crystals below. When each crystal is clicked you add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game when your score equals the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
The value of each crystal is hidden until clicked on.
Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
